Public Protection Unit Database

From May - June 2020, a Discovery into the existing Public Protection Unit Database (PPUD) was undertaken to explore what would be required for PPUD to become a modern, flexible, supportable, and user-centred service that:

  • enables the effective and secure release and recall of offenders
  • diverts patients to the mental health system
  • supports the investigation of complaints and fatal incidents in justice settings
A number of significant issues were identified with the existing service that led to a recommendation that the monolithic PPUD service be deprecated and replaced by a number of modern services, centred around the outcomes that each organisation using PPUD is trying to achieve, built according to modern technology standards.
